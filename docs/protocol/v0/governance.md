# Governance v0

Governance is the process that decides whether a decision request may merge.

## Governance Questions

- Is the evidence sufficient?
- Are key assumptions visible?
- Have objections been considered?
- Are unresolved objections preserved?
- Is authority valid?
- Is the audit complete?
- Is an outcome review scheduled when needed?

## Decision Owners

Decision owners are participants authorized to merge or reject decision requests.

## Reviews

Reviews may:

- approve
- approve with conditions
- request changes
- reject
- comment

## Minority Reports

Dissent must remain attached to the decision record when a decision merges over unresolved disagreement.

Consensus must not erase disagreement.
